bitblitz s1

hackathons: for teens, by teens

We're bitblitz, an coding-based non-profit organization that aims to provide opportunities such as hackathons to high school students. We're hosting bitblitz season 1 as our very first hackathon which will be hosted in the DFW area. Sign up starts soon, so make sure to join our discord server for more updates!

Frequently Asked Questions:

Q1: What is a hackathon?
  A: A hackathon is basically a coding marathon. you are given a set amount of time and you work together with other people to design, create, and present a product within the time period.

Q2: Will food and drinks be provided?
  A: Yep, food and drink are provided, any special food requirements or dietary restrictions like allergies can be written on the form to confirm.

Q3: Are there any costs?
  A: Everything is free! That includes food, registration, and merch!

Q4: Who is eligible to participate?
  A: Anyone currently enrolled in high school is eligible to participate.

Q5: What should I bring?
  A: Just a laptop, a charger, and yourself!

Q6: How many people can be on a team? What if I don't have a team?
  A: Teams can have a miminum of 2 people but only a max of 4. If you don't have a team, we can help you find other teams that you can join during team formation session!

Q7: What if I don't know how to code?
  A: Hackathons are directed towards anyone! We encourage you to join even if you don't have any coding experiences as there are other things you can do such as ui/ux design or help with presentations! We will also have workshops and mentors to help guide you even if you are a beginner in coding!

Q8: This sounds great! Where can I sign up?
  A: Sign up information comes soon so join our discord for more information and updates!
